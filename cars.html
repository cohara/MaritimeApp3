Drogheda Motor Show

Drogheda Motor Show - Brand New Drive 152

19th-21st June 2015 @ The Irish Maritime Festival, Drogheda Port

Facebook: www.facebook.com/BrandNewDrive

Twitter: @BrandNewDrive


DO YOU LOVE THAT “NEW CAR SMELL”? DOES THE GLINT OF SHINY NEW METALLIC PAINT CATCH YOUR EYE? DO YOU LOVE TO COMPARE THE NEW CARS ON THE MARKET TO CHOOSE YOUR FAVOURITE?

If so, you’ll be in heaven at the Drogheda Motor Show!

The Irish Maritime Festival is proud to be the host of the annual acclaimed Drogheda Motor Show. This brilliant Motor Show, now in its 5th year, brings together the Motor Dealers of Drogheda to create a showcase Motoring event.

Bring the kids for fun, free family friendly activities and check out the latest cars and the excellent deals available in the market.

The SIMI (Society of the Irish Motor Industry) award winning Drogheda Motor Show is brought to you by the Motor Dealers of Drogheda, Drogheda and District Chamber and First Citizen Finance.
