Located just 100 metres from the Bru na Boinne Visitor Centre for Newgrange and Knowth .Our brand new 30 bedroom lodge offers a modern but homely atmosphere with excellent facilities for both groups and the independent traveler alike

Enjoy the truly unique and spectacular views from the lodge of the world famous UNESCO site of Newgrange, Knowth and Dowth which are located opposite the Lodge along the River Boyne. Over 600,000 visitors visit this amazing passage tomb every year which is older than the Pyramids in Egypt.
