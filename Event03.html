BOYNE BOAT RACE

Inver Colpa Rowing Club are hosting the annual Boyne Boat Race on Sunday 21st June at 1345hrs. The coastal race will run from Clogherhead beach to the Boyne Fisherman’s Slipway on the Marsh road (approximately14km). A second close quarter race will start at the Boyne Fisherman’s Slipway on the Marsh road at 1545hrs and run to the De Lacy Bridge for a finish (approximately 1.5km).

Drogheda used to be a major centre for competitive rowing. The first Boyne rowing club was established in 1867 holding annual regattas which became one of Ireland’s premier sporting events.Teams from Wicklow and Wexford are already confirmed for the event and last year’s winners Vartry Rowing Club are back to defend their title. Further details about Inver Colpa Rowing Club can be found at www.invercolpa.ie / facebook.com/invercolpa.drogheda


Day: Sunday 21st June 2015

Start: Clogherhead Beach (long race) & Boyne Fisherman’s Slipway (short race)

Finish: Drogheda Port

Time: 13.45 (long race) & 15.45 (short race) Sunday 21st June 2015
