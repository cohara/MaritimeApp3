The Mountain Man Band

The Mountain Man Band are a 2-piece blues and rock band based between Galway and Dublin. Formed in 2014 and have been recording and playing gigs around Galway and Dublin since. We’re all about mixing old bluesy sounds with a newer grittier edge. The band consists of Pádraic Carter on percussion and Declan Keane on guitar/vox.
