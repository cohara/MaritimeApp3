Annie Powderly

Annie Powderly is a Singer/Songwriter based In Drogheda. In the past 3 years, Annie has firmly returned to her musical roots through getting out on the wider gigging circuit and also returning to writing her own songs and performimgat gigs, festivals and events.

In October 2013, Annie released her cd single ‘the Road less travelled’on itunes and also on hard copy. Annie continues to gig as a solo acoustic act all across Leinster. She has also madeappearances on local and national TV and radio and has had a fair few mentions and credits in local and national papers and publications for both her gigging and Wedding expertise.
