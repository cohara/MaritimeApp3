The Conyngham Arms is a beautifully restored 18th Century coaching inn, located in the heart of historic Slane Village.

Our bed and breakfast rate includes luxurious accommodation in one of our beautifully decorated standard, deluxe double or family rooms. Along with a bountiful breakfast in the parlour the next morning.

The hotel is just over 30 minutes’ drive from Dublin International Airport. Taxi services can be arranged to nearby Navan and Drogheda town centres, a little over 20 minutes away.
