Cross Garden

Ganderstown, Clogherhead , Drogheda , Co Louth

Tel: 041 982 2675

Email:


The Cross Garden is situated overlooking the Irish Sea. Located one mile from Clogherhead on the Termonfeckin Road and within a 3 minute walk of the beach. Watersports only in summer.

Two golf courses & pitch and putt nearby. Television and hairdryer in all rooms. Tea / coffee in all rooms. Warm and friendly welcome.
